About...

At Anvil Design & Fabrication we take steel seriously. We build and fabricate a wide range of works, wether you need a stair case for your home or are looking for support on an industrial build, we'll work with you to make your project come to life.
Everything we make is handmade in our Family-run UK workshop with unparalleled attention and devotion.
If you have any questions, ideas or want to talk about a project please get in touch below.

Security

We offer a range of stylish gates and railings to help your home and business stay secure while looking fresh

Staircases

We build bomb proof staircases that are custom measured, built and fitted. Offering a range of colors and finishes.

Balustrades & Handrails

We've learned the best techniques in fabrication to bring personality to your project. We can custom build balconies, balustrades & handrails.

Bespoke

From fully equipped bars and pizza ovens to stylish pergolas, tables, and chairs, we collaborate with you to bring your vision to life and ensure it endures.
